How it works
Set up in minutes. Powerful forever.
Step 01

Install OpenClaw

Run openclaw onboard in your terminal. The wizard walks you through setup on macOS, Linux, or Windows (WSL2).

Step 02

Connect your channels

Link WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord — or all of them. Your assistant appears wherever you already communicate.

Step 03

Add skills & go

Browse ClawHub for skills that match your workflow. Install, configure, and your assistant level-ups instantly.
